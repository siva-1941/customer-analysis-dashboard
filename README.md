# 📊 Customer Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer data to understand behavior, segmentation, and purchasing patterns using Power BI.  
The dashboard helps identify valuable customers, improve retention strategies, and support data-driven business decisions.

This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand customer trends, optimize marketing strategies, and improve overall customer engagement.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
To analyze customer data and uncover insights related to customer distribution, segmentation, and profitability in order to improve business performance and decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Excel  
- MySQL  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified high-value customers using RFM segmentation  
- Analyzed customer distribution across regions  
- Tracked customer purchasing behavior over time  
- Found profitable customer segments  
- Identified patterns in repeat and one-time customers

## 📊 Dashboard Pages
- Customer Overview – Summary of customer metrics and KPIs  
- Customer Analysis – Detailed customer behavior insights  
- Customer Distribution – Regional and demographic distribution  
- Profit Analysis – Profit contribution by customers  
- RFM Segmentation – Customer segmentation based on recency, frequency, and monetary value  
- Time Intelligence – Customer trends over time  
- Insights – Key findings from analysis  
- Business Recommendations – Actionable strategies  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-value customers to increase retention  
- Target low-frequency customers with marketing campaigns  
- Improve engagement strategies for inactive customers  
- Expand in regions with high customer concentration  
- Use RFM segmentation for personalized marketing
